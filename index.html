Aloha Action Plan

  • 2Chronicles20:9-"If disaster should come on us, whether by sword, adverse judgment, pestilence, or famine, let us stand before this house and before you (for your name is in this house) and call to you for help out of our distress, and may you hear and save us."

    Make sure your contact information is updated.

    Make sure your go bags are ready.

    Make sure your supplies will last a few days.

    Know your Group Overseer and BOEs.

    Group Overseer, Coordinator, and Body of Elders are sources of information and help.

    Consider potential problems if you have pets! So keep them safe, if possible.

  • Secure your utilites: Water meter, Gas meter, and Electrical power

    Smell gas in house, leave asap or call utility.

    Fallen power lines, stay in vehicle.

  • Where to go per fire department recommendations:

    Family, friends, Kingdom Hall, and Motels, Hotels, Mass Shelters, Schools, no shelters in our area.

    Call 1-800-733-2767.

    There are no DRC sites nearby.

    FEMA: 1-800-621-3362.

    Follow directions given by the BOEs.

  • Prepare now: Make a plan and Stay informed, Secure home and follow directions of local authorites.

    Have cash and enough food and water for a least three days.

  • Earth quake:

    Drop, cover and hold on and Stay away from windows.

    Flood:

    Evacuate immediately and Never drive or walk through water and secure utilites.

    Fires:

    Follow local authorites directions and BOE's.

    Ice storm:

    Seek help from capable brothers with proper vehicles. Stay warm and connect with family and friends.

    Do not venture out if it is not safe.
